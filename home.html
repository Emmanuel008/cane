About Us

We Are BuniHub

Our mission is to build a stable innovation, technology and entrepreneurship ecosystem in Africa through consultancy and investment. Our long-term vision is to become Africa’s largest consultancy and investment firm. We believe the best way to solve a problem and create impact is by building a business around it.
